Paneer Butter Masala

Rich and Creamy Indian Dish

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add paneer cubes and gently mix to coat them with the sauce.
  5. Pour in cream and simmer for 5 minutes on low heat.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.
